Blueberry Cheesecake



Ingredients

  • 2 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup unsalted sugar

    • For Filling

    • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese
    • 1 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
    • 5 large eggs
    • 1/16-ounce container sour cream
    • 1/4 cup milk
    • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

    Preparation

    Make Crust


    1. Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375 F
    2. Wrap outside the 10-inch-diameter springform pan with heavy duty foil
    3. Combine all ingredients in processor and blend using on/off turns until crumbs are moist
    4. Bake until crumbs begins to brown, about 8 minutes, and transfer crust rack and cool.

    Make Filling

    1. Using electric mixer, beat cream and sugar in large bowl until well blended
    2. Beat in flour and add eggs 1 at a time, beating chust until combined.
    3. Beaty in sour cream, milk, and vanilla
    4. Pour filling into crust
    5. Place springform pan in large roasting pan.
    6. Pour enough hot water into roasting fan to come 1 inch up sides of pan
    7. bake cheesecake until just set in center and top is slightly puffed and golden brown
    8. Turn off oven; keep door closed; let cheesecake stand in oven for 1 hour.
    9. Remove cheesecake from roasting pan and refrigerate until cold.
    10. Cover; refrigerate overnight.

    Make Topping

    1. Stir blueberry spread in large skillet ove rlow heat until melted
    2. Remove from heat and add blueberries
    3. Spoon blueberry mixture over top of cake, spreading evenly.
    4. Chill until cold and transfer cake to platter.
